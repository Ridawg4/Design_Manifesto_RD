Design Manifesto

These principles define how I approach design problems and make decisions.

1. Evidence Over Assumptions

Design decisions should be driven by observation, testing, and user feedback rather than personal preference.

2. Clarity Is Essential

Interfaces should communicate purpose and next steps without requiring explanation.

My Design Process

Iterate
