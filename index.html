Design Manifesto

These principles summarize how my design approach developed over the semester.

1. Evidence Over Assumptions

Design decisions should be guided by observation and testing rather than personal intuition.

2. Clarity Is a Feature

Interfaces should communicate purpose and next steps immediately, without requiring explanation.

3. Iteration Is Essential

Revisiting and refining designs reveals problems that are invisible in initial drafts.

4. Tradeoffs Are Inevitable

Every design balances clarity, persuasion, effort, and context. Recognizing tradeoffs is part of responsible design.

5. Not Every Problem Needs Design

Sometimes the best solution is simplifying or removing technology altogether.

My Design Process

Define
Understand users, goals, and constraints.
Research
Observe real behavior and pain points.
Sketch
Explore ideas quickly and cheaply.
Prototype
Build interactive representations.
Evaluate
Test with users and heuristics.
Iterate
Refine based on evidence.
